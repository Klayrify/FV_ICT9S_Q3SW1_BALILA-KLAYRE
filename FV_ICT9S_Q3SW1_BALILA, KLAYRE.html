Carmen Sandiego is about a young orphan girl with the codename "Black Sheep". She is raised by a group of villains who serve as the faculty at the V.I.L.E. Academy in the Canary Islands. She was discovered abandoned on the side of the road in Buenos Aires, Argentina, about 20 years ago. Until she discovers the organization's actual origins, Black Sheep views the island as her home. By stealing things that V.I.L.E has previously taken and giving them back to their proper owners, she turns rogue, takes the name Carmen Sandiego, and tries to bring down the organization. White-hat hacker named Player, who she has known from her days on the isle and who acts as tech support, along with Zack and Ivy, who assist her on-scene and frequently serve as distractions or operate the different get-away vehicles, makeup Carmen's companions and team (Team Red). Together, they set out to disrupt V.I.L.E.'s plans for global domination while evading the enigmatic secret government organization A.C.M.E., which has set its sights on arresting V.I.L.E and Carmen.
